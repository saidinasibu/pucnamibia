Our History

Founded in 2010, the Unity Party emerged from a grassroots movement seeking to bridge divides and create a more inclusive political landscape.

What began as a small coalition of reform-minded individuals has grown into a national movement with representatives in all regions of the country. Our founders recognized the need for a political force that prioritized national unity over partisan interests.

Over the past decade, we've championed policies that bring people together while respecting our diverse perspectives. Our growth has been organic, driven by citizens who believe in our core principles of justice, prosperity, and unity.

The founding members of Unity Party at our first national convention

Our Core Values

These principles guide every decision we make and every policy we propose.

Inclusivity

We believe in a politics that includes all citizens, regardless of background, and creates opportunities for everyone to participate.

Integrity

We hold ourselves to the highest ethical standards, with transparent decision-making and accountability to the people we serve.

Innovation

We embrace creative solutions to complex problems, combining the best ideas from across the political spectrum.

Compassion

Our policies are rooted in empathy and designed to lift up the most vulnerable members of our society.
